Ready for Fall.


You can still feel beautiful and be fashionable, even in the cold. Check out my various tutorials and lookbooks for the colder seasons. This month I'm featuring the "Snow Queen in Wonderland", which includes a strapless corseted top, skirt, and accompanying winter coat. There are instructions & free patterns for each garment piece. And you don't have to spend a lot! You can coordinate whatever colors you like, but warmer, muted colors look great this time of year! Velvets are trending now.

What's This Stitch?


Have you been overwhelmed with what stitch to use in your projects and when? Well, I have a clear and nifty guide that lays out the most common (and not so common) stitches and when it's convenient to use for specific fabrics. Learn how to properly sew buttons, snaps, and other fun things too. I also include a section on hand-stitching garments for those that may not own a sewing machine. I include alternative options for those without a sewing machine.
